Hello, I'm Martin

I'm a tech speaker and developer from Northampton, England. While I'm naturally quite reserved and shy (very British in that way), I somehow find myself regularly giving talks at technical conferences around the world.

When I'm not coding or speaking, you'll find me playing guitar or appreciating skate culture. This blog is my personal space to share technical articles, conference talks, and the kind of everyday thoughts and experiences I'd typically share on social media (which I've since left behind).

My Interests

Guitar

I've been playing guitar for over 15 years. From blues to rock, I love exploring different styles and techniques. My collection includes a few Fenders and a Gibson Les Paul that I'm particularly fond of.

Currently learning: Jazz chord progressions and improvisation techniques.

Skate Culture

While I don't actually skateboard myself, I've always been drawn to the aesthetic and culture surrounding it. There's something about the style, the music, and the attitude that resonates with me. I enjoy the fashion and design elements that have emerged from skate culture.

Favorite brands: Vans, Thrasher, and independent skate shops
